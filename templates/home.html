GardenGuru
Get Started Overview
What is it? Have you ever tried to start a garden? For a beginning gardener, the amount of information is too intimidating, often minimally relevant.

GardenGuru solves all your research issues. We provide you with plant recommendations based on a picture of the soil you plan to start with. This reduces the research workload on your side.

Simply provide us with a soil picture, and whether you wish to plant a medicinal or edible plant. Then, we will provide you with a wide selection of plants to choose from.

 The Team The GardenGuru Team is composed of Jorgiana, Varun, and Carlos. We are are a group of graduate students at UC Berkeley.

Like many others, when the COVID-19 pandemic struck, we turned to gardening as a hobby. Frustated with how “gatekept” everything felt, the team set out to create GardenGuru.
