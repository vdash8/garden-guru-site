The Data
Our initial dataset was obtained by scraping over 300 Google Images for each soil class.

These image data were then fed to a series of models that predict the soil type.

In our modeling phase, we determined the best performing model to be a NuSVC, a type of support vector classifier (SVC) that improves model generalizability.

How does it work?
GardenGuru can facilitate the garden-building process by providing soil-plant pairings through recommendations. It is as easy as 1, 2, 3!
1. Upload an image of soil into GardenGuru
2. GardenGuru will classify what type of soil you have.
3. After deciphering the soil type, you will be provided with a series of plant recommendations
Start planting!
